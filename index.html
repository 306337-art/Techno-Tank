Multi-purpose TechnoTank

An innovative solution powered by solar energy and smart devices. The TechnoTank alerts communities of floods, filters rainwater into clean water, and provides backup electricity during outages.

🌟 Explore Features

✨ Key Features

🚨 Flood Alert System

Multi-level sensors trigger sirens and send Email alerts via ESP32_email_client module to residents.

💧 Rainwater Filtration

Filters rainwater and transforms it into clean, usable water for the community.

☀️ Solar Power Backup

Generates electricity to power devices and provides backup energy during outages.

🌍 Helping Communities

The Multi-purpose TechnoTank empowers communities by providing life-saving flood alerts, clean water supply, and sustainable solar energy. It is designed to make disaster preparedness more effective and accessible.
